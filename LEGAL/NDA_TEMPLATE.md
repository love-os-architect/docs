### Article 1: Purpose
The purpose of this Agreement is to ensure the confidentiality of proprietary information regarding the "Love-OS" project, allowing the parties to discuss potential collaboration or business engagement.

### Article 2: Definition of Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" shall include all information or material that has or could have commercial value or other utility in the business in which Disclosing Party is engaged. This includes, but is not limited to:
1.  **Technical Data:** Source code, algorithms, architectural blueprints, documentation, and API specifications.
2.  **Strategic Data:** Business models, marketing strategies, and customer/user data.
3.  **Core Philosophy:** Cosmological theories, mathematical models (including the $L=E/R$ framework), and philosophical content not yet released to the public.
4.  Any other information communicated orally, in writing, or electronically that is designated as confidential.

### Article 3: Obligations of Receiving Party
The Receiving Party shall hold and maintain the Confidential Information in strictest confidence for the sole and exclusive benefit of the Disclosing Party. The Receiving Party shall:
1.  Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent.
2.  Use the Confidential Information only for the purpose of evaluating or engaging in the Love-OS Project.
3.  Restrict access to such information to employees or contractors who need to know and have signed confidentiality agreements.

### Article 4: Exceptions
Confidential Information does not include information that:
1.  Is or becomes publicly known through no wrongful act of the Receiving Party.
2.  Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure.
3.  Is required to be disclosed by law or court order (provided the Disclosing Party is given prior notice).

### Article 5: Term
The obligations of this Agreement shall survive for a period of **five (5) years** from the Effective Date.
